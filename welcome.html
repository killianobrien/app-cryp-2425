Welcome to the Applied Cryptography unit. Cryptography is the science of securing information for storage and communication and has a long history in human affairs. Modern cryptography is an essential component of computers and the way they communicate over networks.

In this unit we will study the essential topics from mathematics that will enable us to understand some of the building blocks and main components of modern cryptography.

Our main resource for this unit will be selected chapters from the textbook

  • Stallings, W. (2023) Cryptography and network security. 8th edn. Harlow, United Kingdom: Pearson Education Limited.

Following the above link will take you to the entry for the book on the university's library system from where you have full access to the ebook.
